The Science
Built on the Ebbinghaus Forgetting Curve.

In 1885, Hermann Ebbinghaus proved that memories decay exponentially without reinforcement. Your brain forgets ~50% within 24 hours — and most apps ignore this completely.

Skill Decay Tracker applies this formula to every skill you track. The decay rate adapts based on your actual performance — skills you know well decay slower, fragile skills demand more frequent review. Practice at the right moment, not at a random time.

Thriving
90–100% — keep the streak going
Healthy
70–89% — review soon
Fading
50–69% — practice now
Wilting
30–49% — urgent
Critical
0–29% — skill is dying

Without practice — any skill follows this curve.
